## 🚀 Key Featured Projects

### 🎓 **EduSphere: Unified University Management & Student Support**
*A centralized, role-based platform designed to digitize university academic workflows and automate student support services with AI.*
*   **Tech Stack:** React (Vite), Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, JWT, OpenRouter AI
*   **Key Capabilities:** 
    *   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Customized workspaces for Admins, Faculty, and Students.
    *   **Academic Portals:** Seamless attendance tracking, internal marks publishing, schedules, and analytics.
    *   **AI Support Chatbot:** Integrated instant-assistance chatbot powered by OpenRouter API.
    *   **Grievances & Forms:** Digital workflow handling for queries and requests.
*   🔗 **Links:** [Live Demo](https://edu-sphere-nu-two.vercel.app/) | [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/vaibhavvaid14/EduSphere)

### 🏥 **Hospital Management System (HMS)**
*A professional, full-stack medical workflow management platform designed to streamline doctor schedules, health records, and inquiry systems.*
*   **Tech Stack:** Python, Django 3.2, Django Rest Framework (DRF), SQLite (with PostgreSQL support), Vanilla CSS, JavaScript
*   **Key Capabilities:**
    *   **Doctor Scheduling:** Centralized booking calendar with real-time status tracking (Pending → Confirmed → Cancelled).
    *   **Electronic Health Records (EHR):** Direct and secure management of patient profiles, history, and searchable diagnostics.
    *   **Admin Inquiry Portal:** Integrated public communication portal with read/unread tracking for hospital staff.
    *   **RESTful APIs:** Complete API layer exposing patient, doctor, and appointment data in structured JSON formats.
*   🔗 **Links:** [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Dev-hub05/HospitalManagementSystem)

### 🌿 **MindHaven**
*A dedicated full-stack mental health and wellness platform designed to facilitate personal tracking, resources, and secure support structures.*
*   **Tech Stack:** React, Django, PostgreSQL, Tailwind CSS, JWT Auth
*   **Key Capabilities:**
    *   **Wellness Trackers:** Mood logs and interactive journals to promote daily self-care.
    *   **Resource Center:** Curated mental health articles and guided meditation tools.
    *   **Secure Authentication:** Secure portals for individuals looking to track and store their health statistics safely.
